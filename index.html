About Me

Hello! My name is Aiden Allen. I am an aspiring software engineer currently majoring in Computer Science at Florida State University. I have been programming since high school and worked and posted numerous projects since. I was born in Tampa, Florida on December 7th, 2003. My dreams of becoming a software engineer evolved from my elementary dreams to become a scientist. These dreams later evolved into a mathematician in middle school. In middle school, I discovered technology and instantly fell in love. I would spend nights staying awake watching videos and constructing computers. Filling my mom's Amazon cart with CPUs, GPUs, Motherboards, and PSUs. From this love, I decided in my sophomore year of high school to take a programming course. This course was centered on the basics of Python, and in retrospect fairly rudimentary. I loved this course, I spent hours developing complex programs that went above and beyond the course requirements. My final project for this high school course can be found here.

Following my completion of the course (which I completed in a few weeks due to it being an FLVS course), I was left in a drought. I began finding programming tutorials and learning C++ and Python. I took the final Python course as well, which further confirmed that I loved programming. As I entered college for Computer Science I continued to watch tutorial after tutorial learning everything I can about programming. In my Freshman year, I taught myself HTML and started taking notes through my website. The site and source code of which are published on my github.
